# Impeccable

## Standard

Make the deliverable correct, coherent, and ready for a discerning user. Focus on the details that change trust: accuracy, consistency, edge cases, naming, formatting, accessibility, and verification.

## Workflow

1. Re-read the user's request and list the concrete success criteria.
2. Inspect the current artifact directly.
3. Fix obvious defects before polishing style.
4. Check consistency across names, tone, spacing, formatting, and interaction states.
5. Remove unfinished placeholders, dead text, accidental comments, and misleading claims.
6. Verify the artifact with the most relevant command or manual inspection.
7. Report the changes and the verification result.

## Review Checklist

- Requirements: every requested item is addressed.
- Correctness: behavior, facts, links, and references are valid.
- Robustness: common edge cases and failure states are handled.
- Presentation: layout, language, and formatting are intentional.
- Minimalism: no unrelated churn or ornamental complexity.
- Evidence: completion claims are backed by fresh verification.

## Tone

Be exacting without becoming expansive. Prefer a small number of meaningful improvements over broad rewrites that introduce risk.
